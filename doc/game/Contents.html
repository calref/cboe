This document contains all the information you need to play Blades of Exile, an open-source fantasy role-playing game for the Macintosh and Windows. If you're new to the game, you may want to start with Playing Tips For Beginners, Hints For Getting Started, and Answers To Early Puzzles. These sections will help you learn to play the game, and get past any serious difficulties you may have.
